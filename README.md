## About Me

I work at the intersection of **shop-floor operations, industrial data, and enterprise IT**. My focus is designing practical systems that connect machines, sensors, databases, APIs, and dashboards—then turning that data into clear operational decisions.

- Build and integrate smart manufacturing platforms
- Design data pipelines, APIs, dashboards, and monitoring systems
- Connect PLC, SCADA, sensor, historian, and enterprise data
- Translate factory requirements into scalable digital solutions
- Improve reliability through monitoring, automation, and root-cause analysis
